# Company Project 1 – Website Redesign Brief

## Company Information

**Company Name:** Moss & Associates Attorneys

**Industry:** Legal Services

**Current Website:** https://mosslaw.co.za

---

# How I Found the Company

I discovered Moss & Associates Attorneys through a Google search while looking for South African law firms with an online presence that could benefit from a redesign. The firm's website immediately stood out as an excellent candidate because, while it provides important information about its legal services, the overall presentation does not reflect the professionalism and trust expected from a modern legal practice.

---

# Why I Chose This Company

I chose Moss & Associates Attorneys because I wanted to work on a project that would closely resemble a real-world client engagement. Law firms depend heavily on trust, professionalism, and credibility to attract potential clients, making website design particularly important.

The existing website contains valuable information but lacks the modern user experience expected by today's users. I believed that redesigning their website would allow me to demonstrate my frontend development skills while solving genuine usability and design problems.

---

# Evaluation of the Existing Website

After reviewing the company's website, I identified several areas that could be improved.

## 1. Outdated Visual Design

The website has an outdated appearance that does not communicate the professionalism expected from a legal practice. Typography, spacing, and colour usage make the interface feel old compared to modern law firm websites.

---

## 2. Weak Visual Hierarchy

Important information is not immediately noticeable when the homepage loads. There is no strong hero section to introduce the firm or encourage visitors to take action.

---

## 3. Navigation

Although the navigation is functional, it lacks modern styling and clear calls-to-action. Users should immediately know how to contact the firm or request legal assistance.

---

## 4. Mobile Responsiveness

The website does not provide the same polished experience across different screen sizes. Modern websites should provide an equally effective experience on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

---

## 5. User Experience

The user journey could be improved by presenting information in a clearer order. Visitors should quickly understand:

- Who the company is
- What legal services they offer
- Why they should choose the firm
- How to contact them

---

## 6. Calls-to-Action

The original website contains limited visual emphasis on important actions such as contacting the firm or requesting a consultation.

---

# Goals of the Redesign

The primary objectives of this redesign were to:

- Create a modern and professional appearance.
- Improve the user experience.
- Build a fully responsive website.
- Improve accessibility and readability.
- Strengthen visual hierarchy.
- Highlight the firm's legal services.
- Build trust through cleaner design.
- Encourage potential clients to contact the firm.

---

# Technologies Used

The redesign was built using:

- Next.js (React Framework)
- React
- Tailwind CSS
- JavaScript
- React Icons
- Lucide React

---

# Design Decisions

Several design improvements were introduced throughout the redesign.

## Modern Hero Section

A completely redesigned hero section was created featuring:

- Professional legal imagery
- Strong headline
- Clear call-to-action buttons
- Service highlights
- Improved spacing and typography

---

## Improved Navigation

The navigation bar was redesigned to include:

- Sticky navigation
- Mobile menu
- Better spacing
- Clear navigation links
- Consultation button

---

## Professional Colour Palette

The redesign maintains the company's existing blue branding while introducing:

- Deep navy blue
- White backgrounds
- Gold accent colours
- Neutral grey text

These colours communicate professionalism, trust, and confidence.

---

## Responsive Layout

The website was developed using a mobile-first approach to ensure a consistent experience across:

- Mobile devices
- Tablets
- Desktop computers

---

## Improved Information Structure

Content was reorganised into clearly defined sections:

- Hero
- Practice Areas
- About Us
- Why Choose Us
- Contact
- Footer

This creates a logical flow that guides visitors through the website.

---

# Expected Benefits

The redesigned website provides several improvements over the original.

These include:

- Modern professional appearance
- Better user experience
- Improved readability
- Easier navigation
- Stronger branding
- Better responsiveness
- Clearer calls-to-action
- Increased credibility and trust

---

# Conclusion

This redesign demonstrates how thoughtful user interface design and frontend development can significantly improve a company's online presence while maintaining its existing identity.

The redesigned website reflects the professionalism of Moss & Associates Attorneys and provides visitors with a cleaner, more intuitive, and more engaging experience.
